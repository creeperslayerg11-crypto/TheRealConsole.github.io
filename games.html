Cozy Games
Stardew Valley (Every modern platform) (Players 1-4)
Tired of city life? Well it's a good thing your grandpa gave you his farm in his will, the only bad part, you're gonna have to move to Pelican Town, Where all the characters are hot and creatures roam the caves. Unravel the secrets behind Pelican Town, and finally take down that GOD DAMN WEASEL. Most of the gameplay is the farming, where you water crops and pet animals to get product to sell. Along the way, befriend the residents of the town, and listen to their woes to become friends with them (cause that works somehow), and eventually fall in love with them, unlocking special cutscenes with each bachelor/bachelorette. The world is QUITE LITERALLY your oyster!
Retro Rewind (Players: 1) (PC)
Retro Rewind is a simulator where you play as a VHS store owner within the golden age of the VHS tape. Buy stock and stock your shelves full to draw more customers into your shop to earn more money. There isn't a lot to this one as it just came out and is really still getting update, but the important part is that you can even rewind, and fix the VHS tapes you have. People are gonna find this weird to be within the cozy section, but I think it's a pretty cozy and fun game.

Horror Games
Silent Hill 2 (Original)(PS2, Xbox)
Silent hill 2 is a survival horror game made in 2001. Featuring Main Character: James Sunderland, who gets a letter from his recently deceased wife, who tells him she's within the town of Silent Hill. Explore grand environments, encounter crazy characters and don't forget to pack a flashlight.
Spoilers Ahead

I loved this game when I played it back in 2022. It was a very sureal experience as it was also my first Silent Hill game, and thus, I had no idea what to expect. I ended up loving every part of this game, the puzzles were fun and creative, the combat was just easy enough to get the hang of early, and the creature designs looked FUCKING awesome. Everything about the game feels like it's part of a dream and the voice actors enhance that experience 10 fold, sounding uncanny and almost...not human. Oveerall though, some of the reveals werer not what I was expecting. I'll give it, 9.5/10. Would HIGHLY recommend if you're into horror
