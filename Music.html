Welcome to my list of my favorite artists, albums, and songs! This list is not in any specific order, but is more so an appreciation of the music I love!

  • Chonny Jash: Chonny Jash is a talented indie artist best know for his covers of popular indie songs, like Tally Hall's "Good Day" and "The Bidding" or Cosmo Sheldrakes "Come Along" and "The Moss", though he got his start doing mostly meme mashups of popular memes and songs of the time (around 2019 - 2021). After posting a couple of these mashups on Youtube, he dropped his cover of "The Moss" by Cosmo Sheldrake, which got him noticed by both fans of the original song as well as people in the indie/alternative music scene. He gained a mass followwing when he started his CCCC album (Chonny's Charming Chaos Compendium) which was a collection of "covers" of Tally Hall songs, written to fit a narrative (this will be mentioned later on). Once that was done, he continued posting original songs with some covers here and there.
